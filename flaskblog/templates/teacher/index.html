Self Training Modules

Key Education Foundation offers teacher training for early educators on a variety of topics that help make teaching and learning practices in the classroom developmentally appropriate, play based and effective. Welcome to our online training space. Here you will have access to all our training topics designed as short modules. You can take your learning into your own hands and grow into an excellent early childhood educator.


How does STM work

  • You will have all the modules available to choose from the Courses page
  • Once you select a module, you will be taken to the content area
  • Here you will get to watch videos, read about the topic, answer questions, take quizzes and reflect, to learn more about the topic

  • To learn how to use this platform, watch a video by clicking HERE.
  • It is recommended that you take the modules in the order of the numbering. You will be eligible for an online certificate upon completion of all modules under Introduction to ECE course.
