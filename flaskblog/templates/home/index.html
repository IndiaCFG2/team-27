About Us

Key Education Foundation aims to give the Best Start in Life to every child by providing them a holistic Early Childhood Education. We deliver a strong and sustainable School Readiness program in schools catering to low-income communities. We work with schools, teachers, parents, and children by providing age-appropriate, play-based learning material, participative teacher training, ongoing program support and periodic workshops for parents. Key Education Foundation is a registered Section 8 NGO.
